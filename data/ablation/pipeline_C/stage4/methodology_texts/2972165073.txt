SHADE Dataset We collect SHADE (Synthetic Human Activities with Dynamic Environment), a self-annotated dataset that con- sists of dynamic 3D human skeletons and objects, to learn the prior model for each HOI. It is collected from a video game Grand Theft Auto V with various daily activities and HOIs. Currently, there are over 29 million frames of 3D hu- man poses, where 772,229 frames are annotated. On aver- age, each annotated frame is associated with 2.03 action la- bels and 0.89 HOIs. The SHADE dataset contains 19 Ô¨Āne- grained HOIs for both indoor and outdoor activities.
